Quality Assurance Manager
Digital Green (DG) is an international, non-profit development organization that builds and deploys information and communication technology to amplify the effectiveness of development efforts to affect sustained, social change. The Digital Green system combines technology and social organization to improve the cost-effectiveness and broaden the community participation in existing best practices. It is supported by Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, Ford Foundation, and Government of India. Digital Green's India country office is located in New Delhi with state offices in Bhopal, Bhubaneswar and Bengaluru. It currently works in five states in India, including Bihar, Jharkhand, Orissa, Madhya Pradesh, and Karnataka, in collaboration with a number of partners, like PRADAN and BAIF. It is poised to begin its operations in Andhra Pradesh and expand its work in other states as well.

As DG expands and scales its operations, quality assurance has become increasingly a critical area of focus. We are seeking applications from outstanding professionals with experience in quality assurance to be based with the national team in New Delhi. The position reports to Chief Operating Officer / Chief Strategist.

Key Responsibilities:
  • Develop and implement strategies to ensure high quality in operations as DG scales up its work
  • Nurture and strengthen partnership with area specific NGOs and community groups
  • Develop tools and systems that contribute to effective implementation of standard operating procedures in project activities
  • Ensures delivery of Digital Green Quality Assurance goals and objectives, which are meeting commitments on the field and coordinating overall quality assurance schedule
  • Provides necessary definition, development and deployment of Digital Green quality assurance strategy, addressing all phases of the Digital Green Model
  • Manage the overall expectations pertaining to setting accurate schedules, costs and resources
  • Process necessary project approval and contractual documentation
  • Establish and maintain policy for documentation of Digital Green Services and Products
  • Perform other related duties as required
Qualification and Skills:
  • University degree in Rural Development, Management, Social Sciences or any other relevant discipline
  • Ten to Fifteen years of relevant experience in project/ programme formulation and implementation at national and international levels
  • Familiarity with strategies that assure quality in large scale operations and must continuously improvise quality assurance policies for the organization
  • Demonstrated ability to apply good judgment and decision making skills
  • Excellent writing, communication and negotiation skills; ability to prepare clear and concise reports
  • Excellent leadership, coordination and information management skills
  • Demonstrated analytical skills applied on a wide range of factors, parameters, policies and priorities for the assessment and resolution of complex situations
All positions carry competitive salaries with fringe benefits comparable to leading national level development sector organizations. Desirous candidates should apply with a cover note that highlights their motivation to apply and suitability for the position applied, current CVs and three references to jobs@digitalgreen.org
