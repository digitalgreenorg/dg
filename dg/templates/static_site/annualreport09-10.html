INTRODUCTION We believe that we have established a foundation to exceed the impact that we had originally envisioned
FIELD DEVELOPMENTS We have developed Standard Operating Procedures to provide a framework for the Digital Green system.
PROGRESS Our operations expanded from a small-scale research pilot in one state and with one partner to four states with seven partners in India.
LEARNINGS The initial learning phase represented not only an opportunity for our organization to learn and plan, but also for our team to understand the rural context.
TECHNOLOGY Digital Green works to build technologies and serves as a resource agency to its partners and the communities it works with.
OUR PARTNERS Digital Green works in a variety of different geographies with a diversity of partners and community members.
OUR TEAM Digital Green's core team has grown to eighteen individuals who work through nearly five-hundred partner staff members and community intermediaries who support our efforts on the ground.
FINANCIALS Digital Green's chartered accountants and auditors have developed a financial management system that defines how we maintain our accounts.
