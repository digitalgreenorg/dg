  • pawan@digitalgreen.org

Pawan Ojha studied agriculture sciences and rural development from School of Agriculture Sciences and Rural Development (SASRD), Nagaland University and has more than 14 years of experience in development sector with comprehensive grassroots engagements as well as leadership positions. He has worked in PRADAN with a deep believe in the ability of rural people to take charge of their lives and livelihoods and was engaged with community to develop and manage watershed projects, micro-lift irrigation schemes, women self-help groups and microenterprises in the remote villages of Jharkhand. He has deep interest in promoting sustainable livelihoods for small and marginal farmers, specifically by developing their skills for enhancing productivity and collectivizing them to have better bargaining power in the market. Working with Digital Green would help him in developing his knowledge in ICT, and use it for the purpose of capturing the positive experiences and utilizing them for replication for reaching large number of families by adopting the good practices for improving their lives.
