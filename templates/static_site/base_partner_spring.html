SPRING
SPRING is a United States Agency for International Development (USAID)-funded five-year project. SPRING's focus is the reduction of anemia and stunting among women and young children through the expansion of effective social and behavior change communication (SBCC) and nutrition-sensitive programs at scale. Digital Green is working with SPRING in the Indian state of Odisha.
