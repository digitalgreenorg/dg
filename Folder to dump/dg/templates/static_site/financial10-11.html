Financials
Digital Green's chartered accountants and auditors in the U.S. and India have developed a robust financial management system that defines how we maintain and administer our accounts within and between the countries in which Digital Green operates. Digital Green, as an organization, has transitioned from an embedded approach in which our team operated from the field offices of our partners to a hub-and-spoke model in which our team operates from regional offices and provides training and backstopping support to our partners through regular field visits.
