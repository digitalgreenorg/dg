The Game: Wonder Village
Wonder Village is a game about turning a sparsely populated impoverished settlement, which you've just discovered, into an absolute wonder. The people there are constantly rooting for you, and some are there as Village Gurus to help you achieve your goal. The further you get the levels, the more Village Gurus are made available to you. Quests pop in and out on the top-left panel to serve as a guide.
