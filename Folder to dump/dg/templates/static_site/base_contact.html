Contact Us
Do connect with us! You can help us identify and set up collaborations with partners. You can help set up the Digital Green system in other places. You can help contribute content. You can help with other technical aspects of Digital work (such as setting up a community radio extension of Digital Green, or developing software, or working on novel electricity-generation solutions).

You can experiment with applying the similar Digital Green approach to other areas of work (such as education, health care, and political activism). You can contribute money. You can help connect us with funders. You can help spread awareness of Digital Green by word of mouth.

Subscribe to our 'Friends of Digital Green' mailing list by sending a request to contact@digitalgreen.org Updates are sent once a quarter. We encourage you to join our mailing list to learn more about our activities and participate in a global exchange of ideas on how we can better design and develop the Digital Green system.
